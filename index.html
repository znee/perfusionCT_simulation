Concept

Convolution: The tissue concentration curve $C_t(t)$ is the convolution of the Arterial Input Function (AIF) and the Residue Function $R(t)$, scaled by CBF.

$$C_t(t) = CBF \cdot [AIF(t) \otimes R(t)]$$
